Here on the Greenville university Esports team we really emphasizes team work. Currently alot of our games require teams of 3 to 5 people. We make sure that when teams of 3 to 5 are all on the same page and equal in skill so they work well together. Making sure they work together and have fun together is a big part of having a great team.






The Esports team runs on communication skills, all the way from in game and out. Communication is a big part of life, with friends to coworkers. On the team we make sure there isnt any miscommunication on the game field, to help out we practice call outs and the names of the call outs, so we stay on the same page in game.






Who wants to play the game with strangers? No one, here we play with friends, and friends only. Not saying we dont let new people in, but when you decide to join we try to make sure you feel as welcomed as possible. Making sure you leave with new friends, maybe a new best friend.






Greenville being a christen University, we bring God into everything. So, to start every game we begin with prayer. Now we dont pray for a win, we pray for the well being for our opponents and for ourselves. We also pray for the greatness of the competition and the enjoyment of honoring christ with our play.
